Cloud Storage

1 Terabyte is 1000 Gigabytes, which is enough space to hold:

  • 1 billion average sized emails
  • 3.6 Million average sized Microsoft Word documents
  • 1 Million high res jpg Picture Files
  • 600,000 mp3 Songs
  • 300 Hours of Video
  • 1000 Copies of the Encyclopaedia Britannica.

The Terabyte allocated to your business is shared between your Files and Folders, SQL Databases and Exchange Email Systems. It is your choice how you divide the available storage.
